Main Content Area

This is the main content of the webpage. It's built to be flexible and adapt to different screen sizes. On a desktop, this content sits next to the sidebar. As the screen gets smaller, the layout will shift, and this section will likely take up more space or stack on top.

The `main-content` and `sidebar` are part of the larger Grid layout, demonstrating a clean separation of concerns for the page structure.
